The Problem

EduPay exists to simplify school-related payments for Kenyan parents by consolidating multiple payment types (fees, meals, uniforms, trips, exams) into a single, transparent digital platform. It solves the problem of fragmented payment channels, lost receipts, and lack of visibility in school financial transactions.
